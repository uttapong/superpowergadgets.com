Company Profile

Power Gadgets is a specialist manufacturer of power banks that provides various solutions for power banks in terms of power efficiency and durability. We provide customers plenty of attractive designs for their satisfaction.

Our core competence lies in our people. We have built the infrastructure that stimulates and protects people’s creativity. Our dynamic team of QC and engineering professionals knows how to work creatively to make the whole company more productive with reduced production defects rate from scratch to finish, and increased product added-value via continuous design optimization.  

We look forward to sharing product knowledge and experience with our buyers, We expect to listen to your requirements, understand your business and learn from you or to offer you our hand.

Vision

To emerge as the largest Solution Provider for Mobile Accessories for Domestic as well International Market, by use of Innovative Energy Efficient Products at very Affordable prices. 

Mission

To provide quality & affordable mobile products to enhance people’s lifestyles

Core Values

To make our people’s dream come true, To fulfill our commitment to our customers

Strategy

Complete Ownership Control and Total Cost Management. We are in full control of the products from inception to launch, and everything else in between. We develop our own designs by internal R&D and also considering customer feedback. This enables us to quick respond to market demands and customer needs, allowing a faster time to market. Meanwhile, we protect and support our customers stand higher in their markets with quality consistence, exclusive solutions and competitive prices. In this way, the switching cost is reduced both for customer and us. Ultimately the preciseness with which we drive the product development cycle results in continuous improvements, flawless products and fewer support calls.

Customer Driven Innovation

Customer driven innovation is the key to our continued success.   We are open-minded and know that the best ideas can come from anyone, in particular our customers.   Customer driven innovation enables an organic process of continuous evolution and personalization of our products and services and an ever-improving experience for you, the customers. 

Customer Satisfaction= Customer Recommendation

We are in constant contact with our customers. We continuously translate our customer's needs, wants or difficulties into improved products and services.   We aim to make our customers so satisfied that they recommend us to everyone.
